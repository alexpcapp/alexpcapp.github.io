Alexander Cappelen

I am masters student in the sociology programme at the Department of Sociology, University of Copenhagen.

My academic interests are based on computational sociology or social data science, where I mainly focus on the contemporary far-right movement. I am interested in quantiative methods, including causal inference, machine learning, analysis of social networks and using unstructured data as well as other data from social media.

As of February 2023, I have been affiliated with the Copenhagen Center for Social Data Science (SODAS) as a teaching assistant. I have functioned as TA for the courses Advanced Social Data Science I and Digital Methods until December 2023.

In the fall of 2023, I wrote my master's thesis "Parlarchy in the UK". I investigated the relationship between activity on the far right social media Parler and hate crime in the UK, earning a top grade after the oral defence.
