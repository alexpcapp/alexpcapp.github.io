Politicians Publishing Books

Summer 2023

Alexander Cappelen

This mini-project asks whether members of the Danish parliament tend to publish more or less political or ideological books over time. I did not manage to find any data source myself, so using the Danish Wikipedia, I went through the Wikipedia page for each member of parliament since 1987 to figure out if they had published books before each election since 1987. For the two most recent elections, 2019 and 2022, there have the fewest number of published politicians since around 2000. However, the data source of Wikipedia does not always provide a systematic data collection, which means not all data points are accurate.

By Alexander Cappelen, based on Hugo Researcher
