Project Description

The Speeches

For this website, I collect speeches on a yearly basis. The source is the website www.dansketaler.dk. The collected speeches are available as one .csv-file here, which includes the speech, the author, the year, and the context of the speech (New Years Speech, speech at an event, etc.). The speech-dataset is updated each year following the upload of the Royal New Years Speech. The update adds all speeches by King Frederik and Queen Margrethe held in the past year.

All New Year speeches by Queen Margrethe II are available in Danish from www.dansketaler.dk. With Python I scrape the speeches and organise them into a structured dataset after removing HTML tags and formatting the text (removing /n, ensuring whitespace after period, and so on).

About the project

Among other projects, I developed this project for my own interest in data analysis and coding. The specific idea came in January 2024 after Queen Margrethe announced her abdication. I first developed the individual analysis of New Year Speeches by Queen Margrethe over time. Later on, I decided to develop a framework for adding similar analyses by King Frederik for his coming New Year Speeches. Comparing the two sounded like an interesting idea to me, while also being a feasible project to develop single-handedly with a few hours a week.

About me

My interest in text data and data analysis comes from my time in university. I have a Master in Sociology from the university of Copenhagen, where I first developed my interest in coding and data analysis.
