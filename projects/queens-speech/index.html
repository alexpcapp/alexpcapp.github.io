The Royal Danish Family have addressed the nation on New Year's Eve since 1941. Traditionally, the speech goes over important events during the year, regarding both the Denmark and the Royal Family itself. On New Year's Eve 2023, Queen Margrethe announced she would abdicate in the beginning of 2024, leaving the throne to her son. I have collected all New Year Speeches of Queen Margrethe II to a dataset. The intention for this site is to present interesting knowledge about the speeches.
